Florida:Salsa


Description

Florida salsa is a vibrant and refreshing dish that reflects the state's diverse cultural influences and abundance of fresh produce. Typically made with locally sourced ingredients like ripe tomatoes, sweet bell peppers, onions, and a hint of jalapeño for spice, this salsa stands out for its tropical twist. Florida salsa often incorporates fruits like mango, pineapple, or citrus, which add a burst of sweetness and balance the heat. The combination of tangy lime juice, fragrant cilantro, and a touch of salt ties the flavors together, creating a perfect blend of sweet, spicy, and tangy.

This salsa is a versatile dish enjoyed as a dip with tortilla chips or as a topping for grilled seafood, chicken, or tacos. It embodies Florida's coastal essence, often used in beachside cookouts and outdoor gatherings. The tropical fruit additions reflect the state's rich agricultural offerings, making it not only a flavorful accompaniment but also a healthy and refreshing choice. Whether served at a summer party or as part of a casual meal, Florida salsa is a bright, colorful, and zesty dish that captures the essence of the Sunshine State.

Ingrediants

  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes
  • ½ onion, finely diced
  • ¼ cup canned sliced green chiles, or to taste
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • ½ lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps :

  1. Place tomatoes, onion, green chiles, cilantro, lime juice, garlic, and salt in a blender or food processor. Blend on low to desired consistency.
  2. Try it with a plain cracker.
